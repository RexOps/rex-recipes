=head2 Cloudera Hadoop Framework

With this Rex-Module it is possible to install, configure
and manage your whole Hadoop-Cluster and some Hadoop-Tools.

The included Rex-Modules are tested and active used on productive
Hadoop-Clusters.

In the future even more Hadoop-Tools will be integrated as
Rex-Modules.
